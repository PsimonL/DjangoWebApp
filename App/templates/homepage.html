Welcome to Currency Converter!

Purpose:

Currency converter was build within university classes. Aiming for acquaintance with Web Apps topic.


How to use it:

Basically it's just 3 pages; homepage, currency converter and charts. First one is just introduction as you can see. Second one is currency converter between 5 currencies. Based on Polish National Bank API.

Requirements, technical side:

I have used Pycharm as my programming environment. Backend was created in Django and Front in vanilla JS and CSS3 as well as HTML5. Prepared for usage of sqlite database. Three buttons at the beginning of every single page is form of navigation.
